# Wisp-Walkers
Hello everyone! Thank you for stopping by and for taking a look at Wisp Walkers, my first platform game!

In this magic world, you play as Remi, an energetic girl who dreams about leaving the monotony of her house and experiencing a new and exciting story! One day, she suddenly wakes up in the magic land of Wisp World, where her dreams can become true, and she can enjoy the magic world she's always dreamed of!

You'll meet old friends and new ones, who will help you explore deeper in the world and familiarize with every special trick the Wisps have to offer! Hope you enjoy the ride as much as I will enjoy making it!
